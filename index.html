About Us

We are a team of emerging tech professionals who have recently completed the Cloud Computing bootcamp with Generation Australia.

Our Peer Learning Group (PLG6) has developed this website as our final project of the course.

Get to Know: Your Future Tech Trailblazers

From 'You're on mute!' to Mastering Mute: our team's digital glow-up in the Zoom-iverse! #LevelUp

As we approached our final project, we knew we had a wealth of skills acquired from our Cloud Computing bootcamp to present. The brief was flexible: we could showcase these skills in any manner or medium that suited us.

Collectively, PLG6 decided to take the Python projects we had crafted earlier in the course, and weave them into a cohesive narrative in a single, static website portfolio.

This website serves as an effective tool to demonstrate our knowledge across various platforms:

  • Azure - hosting with Azure App Service.
  • GitHub - code repository.
  • Bootstrap - front end with BootstrapeMade | Impact template.
  • Jira - Scrum documentation.
  • Confluence - project documentation.

Teamwork has been the backbone of our studies at Generation Australia. Through the Behavioural Skills and Mindsets (BSM) classes, we refined our adapatability, enhanced our client focus, improved our orientation to detail, and bolstered our teamwork capabilities. Equipped with these skills we were able to strategise effectively throughout our project which was fundamental to the overall project success.

Project Presentation

On 9th June 2023, PLG6 presented the completed project to their cohort and instructors.
